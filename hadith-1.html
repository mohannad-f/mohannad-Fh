English Explanation:

Dear reader, this noble hadith establishes a clear standard for excellence and virtue among people. The Prophet (peace be upon him) did not say that the best of you are the wealthiest, strongest, or most beautiful, but rather "those with the best character."

Good character encompasses truthfulness in speech, trustworthiness in dealings, mercy toward creation, patience in the face of harm, forgiveness when able to retaliate, humility with people, thinking well of others, and cheerfulness in countenance.

The Prophet (peace be upon him) had the best character among all people, to the extent that Aisha (may Allah be pleased with her) described him saying: "His character was the Quran." He is the role model and highest example of good character.

So, dear brother, if you want to be among the best of people, make your primary concern the improvement of your character, for good character can elevate its possessor to the rank of one who fasts and prays at night, and it is the heaviest thing in a believer's scale on the Day of Judgment.
